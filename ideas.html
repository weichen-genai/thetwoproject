Series one

TLog

Thinking in Task, Workflow, Organization

TLog is the conceptual stream: short videos that sharpen the logic of T-W-O, explain the missing middle of workflow redesign, and clarify where AI changes are operational versus merely local.

Topics include task decomposition, workflow bottlenecks, validation burden, redesign logic, and ownership.

Series two

Field Notes

AI-Native Experiments

Field Notes tracks experiments, tools, and observations from trying to work in more AI-native ways. It is the practical companion to the framework, closer to the edge of what is changing right now.

Topics include workflow experiments, tool behavior, design choices, and what breaks when AI is inserted into real systems.
