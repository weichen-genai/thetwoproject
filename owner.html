About

Organizational change, disguised as an AI initiative

The Owner follows Ethan, a transformation lead at Northstar Solutions, as GenAI pilots collide with workflows, incentives, and the question of who actually redesigns the work when tasks move.

For leaders, operators, and builders who care why demos succeed and production stalls—it is the narrative companion to the T-W-O framework (task, workflow, organization), without slide decks or jargon.

Full manuscript: 48 chapters in three parts. Public preview: 8 chapters. Official launch: May 2026.

Part I (1–16)

Speed without workflow change—and the limits of that pattern.

Part II (17–32)

Validation, exceptions, sequencing, and the cost of unclear ownership.

Part III (33–48)

Who can redesign how work moves—not only who ships another model.
