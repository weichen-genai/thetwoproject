Mission

Research and practice at the intersection of AI and organizational change.

The lab's work spans academic research, a business novel, and applied experiments with AI-native workflows. The goal is to close the gap between what AI can do at the task level and what organizations actually need to change to benefit from it.

The T-W-O Project explores how AI transforms tasks, workflows, and organizations.
